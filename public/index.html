About Aumentar

Aumentar is all-inclusive software which helps our customer to solve their business challenges by giving the wide range of products - Point of Sale, Order Management, Feedback, Inventory Management and Reporting tools. Aumentar software is customizable for different types of restaurant formats. Aumentar provides the cloud based infrastructure which gives real time access and helps to manage the business with ease.
